Class Notes: The basics of the Web and HTML


HTML is what glues everything together Web has 30 billion pages Major pieces of the web: you, your computer, the internet and servers HTTP-the main protocol of the web HTML: hypertext markup language. HTML is made up of text content, markup (what it looks like), references to other documents (video, images). Links to other documents. HTML is made up of tags contents . the first is the opening and the last is the closing element. b tag is for bold. You need an opening and closing tag. em is for emphasis. It italicizes the text.
